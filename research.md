# Research

### Sample Size Calculations for SMART with Skewed Outcomes
Joint work with Dr. X. Yan and Dr. B. Chakraborty.  
Focuses on deriving power- and precision-based formulas for SMART designs under skew-normal outcomes.

### Kernel Stein Discrepancy in MCMC
With Dr. Dootika Vats (IITK).  
Investigated computational tradeoffs in Stein-thinning for bias-corrected MCMC output.

### Semiparametric GE Regression for Rainfall Trends
Mentored by Dr. Arnab Hazra.  
Modeled long-term rainfall trends in Western Ghats using a novel GE regression framework.
