#  Publications

---

- **Semiparametric GE Regression for Rainfall Trends**
  
  *Joint work with Dr. Arnab Hazra (IIT Kapur)*
  
  Conducted a comprehensive statistical analysis of precipitation data across the Western Ghats (1901–2022). Designed a semiparametric regression framework based on the Generalized Exponential (GE) distribution and developed a novel penalized complexity prior for its shape parameter to model long-term rainfall trends.

  Dey, A. and Hazra, A. (2023+). A semiparametric generalized exponential regression model with a principled distance-based prior for analyzing trends in rainfall. Under review in Journal of Applied Statistics (JAS). Link- https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.03165

# Research Projects

Below is a selection of projects I've worked on for the past few years.

---

- **Sample Size Calculations for SMART with Skewed Outcomes**
- 
  *Joint work with Dr. X. Yan and Dr. B. Chakraborty (Duke-NUS Medical School)*
  
  Developed novel sample size calculation formulas—both precision-based (for pilot SMARTs) and power-based (for confirmatory SMARTs)—under skew-normal outcome distributions. These formulas enable more accurate planning of SMART designs in clinical trials where outcome skewness is common.

---

- **Kernel Stein Discrepancy in MCMC**
- 
  *Supervised by Dr. Dootika Vats (IIT Kanpur)*
  
  Explored Stein-based discrepancy measures for assessing convergence in MCMC algorithms. Introduced Stein-thinning as a sub-sampling strategy to reduce computational overhead while maintaining accuracy. Extended the methodology to biased MCMC chains, mitigating bias while improving efficiency.
